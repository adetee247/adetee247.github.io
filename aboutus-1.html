ABOUT US

Gasoline Associates

Gasoline Associates International Limited is an indigenous company engaged in the production of oil and petrochemical products. Gasoline Associates International Limited took a bold step to provide a first class Refinery and Petrochemical facility with a 100,000 tons Per Day (BPD) capacity of crude oil It comprises of a 100,000 BPD crude oil refinery consisting of two stainless steel modules of 50,000 BPD each, expandable to 250,000 BPD. The core refinery process comprises a gasoline fuel oil refinery, with Naphtha stabilizer and spliing; naphtha desulphurizer and catalytic reformer, kerosene hydro treated; liquid catalyst cracker; gas plant; alkylaon; and delayed Coker capable of processing Nigerian light crude (Bonny, Forcadors, Qua Iboe, etc.) with general specifications of 33-37 API and Sulphur content of 0.10% by WT. In addition, the project would comprise the following components: Gasoline Associates Int’ LTD will ultimately be the leading provider of quality petrochemicals and related products by leveraging its proven technologies and harnessing its human capital for the benefit of its stakeholders..
